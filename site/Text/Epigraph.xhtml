A Story to Tell

We all have a story to tell, our own story, unique to each of us. It is a story, that is important because it is unique. It is precious to us, to God and, if we are fortunate, to those who love us.
I am fortunate.
Quotation from "A faith worth sharing? A church worth joining?"
by Cyril Ashton and Jack Nicholls.
Darton Longman & Todd. London
